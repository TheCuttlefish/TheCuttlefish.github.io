All Systems Go - Development Notes

About

Project developed with Nicola Zamboni and Olga Guseva.

A prototype based on 60's space race and limited gameplay space (Diameter of 4726px). Player is in control of a spaceship, player has to explore and discover planets and moons within inner solar system. Player can explore planets and moons by navigating a full circle around them. Player can see the progress via the map presented around the ship when she enters a gravity field. The challenge is presented in the form of asteroids, comets and other celestial objects.
